Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Interflow ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our fasting tracker application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and display name when you create an account
  • Fasting Data: Start times, end times, duration, and notes for your fasting sessions
  • Health Data: Optional mood, energy levels, and weight entries you choose to log
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app to improve our services

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the fasting tracking service
  • Display your fasting statistics and progress
  • Enable features like achievements, leaderboards, and sharing
  • Send notifications about your fasting schedule (if enabled)
  • Improve and optimize our application

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Firebase, a Google Cloud service. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Your fasting data is associated with your account and is not shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure sign-in via Google, email, or social providers
  • Firebase Firestore: For storing your fasting data
  • Google Fonts: For typography

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Export your fasting data (CSV, JSON)
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of leaderboard participation

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete all your data at any time through the Settings page. Upon account deletion, all your data is permanently removed from our servers.

Children's Privacy

Interflow is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
